Commissioner Service

Every unit is assigned a commissioner. A commissioner is a seasoned volunteer scouter who has deep scouting knowledge over years of experience in scouting leadership roles. A commissioner is you scouting mentor, he or she can answer questions, suggest strategies for solving unit problems or just listen.

If you don't know who your commissioner is, please contact the district commissioner and he or she will put you into contact with your current commissioner.
