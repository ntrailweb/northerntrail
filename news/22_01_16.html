Experiences of a Lifetime

Thirty years ago this spring, my son and I went on a Philmont trek. The memory is still fresh in my mind today. My son recently recalled the experience and he too savors the memories. Not every scouting activity may reach the level of Philmont, but all of them are memorable in their own ways. Special in that they are out of the ordinary. They can inspire, humble and delight. Scouting is special, seize the moment, before you know it, decades will have passed.

Camporee, March Madness

TRJ Madness
March 25-27 Check in starts at 6:00 on Friday. Check out by 10:00 am Sunday.

Please join Northern Trail District for our spring camporee, TRJ Madness. All the fun you expect at camporee with the addition of Basketball skills competition in the afternoon, trivia and other basketball themed madness. Plenty to do including round robin patrol activities in the morning and in the afternoon open areas and competitions including open ranges, root beer run, and climbing. Saturday night campfire will feature awards for the day's activities given to top performing individuals, patrols, and troops. Order of the Arrow Call out will be available for participating troops Saturday night and we encourage all Brotherhood Eligible Order of the Arrow members to participate in the Brotherhood walk. Red Cross CPR class will be available Saturday afternoon.

Leaders Guide and additional information will be available by February 1st.

A Celebration of Scouting

Please join us at the Circle Ten Council Annual Recognition Banquet at the Plano Event Center (2000 E. Spring Creek Parkway, Plano) on Thursday, March 24, 2022 at 6:30 p.m.

The Annual Recognition Banquet is held each year to recognize Eagle Scouts and the volunteers of the Circle Ten Council. The highlight of the evening will be the presentation of the 2021 Max Carrier Class of Eagle Scouts and the 2021 Silver Beaver Awards, the highest volunteer recognition presented on the council level. Click here to see the current Class of Silver Beavers. You may access a flier for the banquet to share with your units here

We are excited to host an in-person event this year and have confidence in the COVID 19 protocols the Plano Event Center has in place for the safety of our guests. Tables will be set for 8. The price this year will be $60 per person or $480 for a table. Registration is now open and will close on March 8th. You may register here: scoutingevent.com/571-2022Councilbanquet. Feel free to reach out to Leigh Anne LeBlanc, council banquet chair, with any questions or concerns at leighcat3@gmail.com.
