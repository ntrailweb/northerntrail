Twilight Camp is around the corner!

Cub Scouts have a blast at Twilight, get signed up today. June 9-13, 2014 (4 PM - 8:30 PM). Registration is open for Scouts and Walking Den Leaders! The cost for camp is $55 per Cub Scout, $30 for Tigers, $10 for Adults and $5 per person for the Friday Webelos Overnight. Price goes up May 1st, so register early! Read more.
