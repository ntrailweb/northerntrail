Twilight Camp Begins Monday June 9th.

Time is running out, please register ASAP.

Twilight Camp is the perfect way for your cubs to have a lot of fun while advancing on their Cub Scout trail! Earn belt loop and rank advancement while getting out of the house for a week with their friends. Activities include BB guns, archery, crafts, Scout skills, field games and sports, and more.

Staff Needed!

We still have a critical need for more adult Staff as well as older junior staff. We are short 4 adults right now. We have openings in Tiger Sports and Games, Wolf Sports and Games, Bear Scout Skills and Outdoorsman. Even if you are only available to help for one or two days, that will really help us. The program is entirely planned so no background is needed. This camp will not run without your help. Please go to www.ntrail.org/twilight and register as staff.
