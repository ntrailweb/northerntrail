Camporee Planning Meetings

Camporee will be held March 18-20 at Camp Constantin. This is a annual event which gets better every year. If your unit has not participated lately, now is the time to put it on your calendar and help make it a success. Camporee is work of volunteers and this work must be done not only at Camporee, but during planning meetings in advance of the event. Scout and adult volunteers are needed.

Step up to the plate and come to the next planning meeting on Wednesday, Feb. 24, 7:00pm, Room 2B in Bartula Family Life Center, First United Methodist Church of Richardson. Volunteering at Camporee is a rewarding experience and the time invested is well spent.

For more information, visit our website: http://ntrail.org/content/camporee-2016

Wilderness First Aid

Wilderness First Aid is required for all BSA High Adventure bases - Philmont, Northern Tier, Sea Base, and Summit - and recommended for all scouting activities beyond one hour of medical help.
Class dates: Feb 5-7, Apr 1-3, Apr 29- May 1, and May 20-22
Classes: Friday, 6:30 - 10:30 pm; Saturday, 8 - 4:30 pm; Sunday, 12:30 - 4:30 pm
Cost: $75
CPR/AED is required and will be offered the Wednesday before each class
