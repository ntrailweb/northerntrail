Help your Scouts achieve Eagle rank

The Eagle rank is different than all other Scouting ranks. It is the highest, most recognized award in Scouting and has a different process than other ranks which varies from Council to Council and District to District. Come to Round Table this month to learn how our district, Northern Trail, handles that process.

In a nutshell, Eagle advancement in Northern Trail is a partnership between units and the District. A District representative, called an Eagle Designee, must approve the Scout's project proposal and sit on the Eagle board of review. You can find the list of Northern Trail Eagle Designees here. Only the scouters on this list can approve Eagle Projects and represent the District at Eagle boards of review in Northern Trail.

Come to Round Table on March 9 to learn more about this process and how to help your Scouts achieve the Eagle rank.

Venturing Rendezvous

Venturing Rendezvous is scheduled for March 18, 2023 at Camp Wisdom. Camping is available for Crews and Ships wishing to do so, just reach out once you are registered for Rendezvous and we'll assign you to a campsite.

Rendezvous is the spring event which brings all the Venturers, Sea Scouts, and Explorers together for fun and fellowship. If you are 14 but not part of a Crew or Ship, reach out to a nearby Crew and come as their guest. If you need to find a Crew or you have questions, please feel free to contact Joe Tavares, VOA Advisor for Circle Ten (and a member of this District) for more details.
