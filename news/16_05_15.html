Staff needed for Twilight Camp

Twilight Camp cannot happen unless we have volunteers to implement the program. If you can volunteer for every afternoon, great, but we can use volunteers for specific days as well. Those of us who have staffed Twilight camp know that the experience is very rewarding. It's fun and you have the satisfaction of making a difference in the lives of the participants. The areas that we need staff are Scout Skills, Crafts, Webelos Adventures, registration(helping Barbara), the Wood Shop and all Ranges. Please sign up here and let us know what area you would like to work. Both adult and youth staff are needed. Face-to-face YPT is required for staff 18 years and older. Sign up today!

Oak Leaf Spring 2016, 5/21-5/22

Oak Leaf is a junior leader experience designed to supplement Troop level training and prepare Scouts for NYLT (National Youth Leadership Training). It is for Boy Scout youth, First Class Rank or above who will be serving in a leadership capacity within their Troop. Minimum age is 12 years old at time of course. Registration will close Wednesday! Space is limited, don't miss out!

National Youth Leadership Training (NYLT)

National Youth Leadership Training is an intense, 5 ½ day, outdoor leadership training experience for youth troop, team and crew leaders. The course goal is to prepare young men and women to become superior leaders in their troop, crew, district, council, and other organizations they belong to now, and in the future.   Continue reading
