Loyal

From the Scout Handbook - "A Scout is loyal. A Scout is true to his family, friends, Scout leaders, school, and nation. Loyalty means to be faithful. When we say a Scout is Loyal, it means he or she remains steadfast in adherence to what is right." Loyalty is a key element providing direction for character. Leaders, remind your scouts what it means to be loyal.

Roundtable Thursday May 13, 2021

Bring the family and come join us as we celebrate the 60th Anniversary of Alan Sheppard becoming the first American in space with our STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering and Mathematics) themed Roundtable. We'll be launching 2-liter soda bottle rockets throughout the entire evening as different groups have displays on different STEM activities that scouts and units can do. Invited groups include the Dallas Geological Society, the AT&T Professional Engineers, Dr. Kenneth Berry (Lead Author of the Robotics Merit Badge Pamphlet), Dallas Paleontological Society and the Richardson Wireless Klub. Twilight Camp 2021, June 14-18, 2021

WANTED: Cub Scouts who want to have fun. Twilight Camp is June 14-18 at Richardson’s Breckenridge Park. Join your friends and get a start on advancement. Register now here.

Twilight camp is a week-long late afternoon, early evening event for Cub Scouts. The theme for Twilight Camp this year is "Weird Science." Lots of fun activities are planned in a COVID safe environment. This event will be held, as in the past, at Breckinridge Park in Northeast Richardson. BB guns, archery, crafts, games, egg drop, swaps and more. Registration now available. Check out pictures from past Twilight Camps here. Another benefit is that as an adult, you will enjoy the activity and meet other parents dealing with the many of the same challenges you are facing.

National Youth Leadership Training

National Youth Leadership Training is an exciting, action-packed program designed for councils to provide youth members with leadership skills and experience they can use in their home troops and in other situations demanding leadership of self and others.

The NYLT course centers around the concepts of what a leader must BE, what he must KNOW, and what he must DO. The key elements are then taught with a clear focus on HOW TO. The skills come alive during the week as the patrol goes on a Quest for the Meaning of Leadership. Learn more.
