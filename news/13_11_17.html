Retired Pinewood Derby Track for Sale

At the November Cub Scout Roundtable, we ran a 5-car, 20-heat pinewood derby demonstration. The track we used came from Pack 893, where it created many happy memories until it was replaced with a shiny alumninum model in 2007, and has been collecting dust ever since. P893 is asking $100 obo. If you are interested in giving this track a new home, please contact Cub Rountable Commissioner, Steve Sweeney at ssweeney@ntrail.net More information
