Twilight Camp June 5-9, 2017

Walking Den Leaders

Twilight Camp would not be possible without walking den leaders.

  • All walking den leaders must be registered on Camp Master and have a current Face to Face Youth Protection Card.
  • Walking Den Leaders must enter their scheduling information on-line so we can assign you to groups.
  • A medical form is required for all adults and scouts attending camp.
  • Walking Den Leaders must fill out and submit an "Adult In Camp State Compliance Form."
Camp Master: circle10.camp-master.com/ Look for Northern Trail Twilight Camp.
On-line Scheduling Form: northerntrail.circleten.org/twilight-camp-adult-group-assignments
Adult in Camp State Compliance Form: http://northerntrail.circleten.org/sites/circleten.org/files/media/district/nt/twilight_2014_volunteerinfosheet_fillable.pdf
Medical Form: http://www.scouting.org/filestore/healthsafety/pdf/680-001_ab.pdf

Date: Wednesday, May 31st from 8am to 9pm.
Location: Barbara Richer's Home
Address: 1512 Summertree Ct, Richardson, TX 75082
If you are not able to come on Wednesday, please contact Barbara Richer at 214-478-3972 barbara.richer@yahoo.com to arrange another pickup time prior to camp.
