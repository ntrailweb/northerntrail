Annual Re-Charter

If your re-charter is not complete, very soon you will be dropped from the system. This means that for you unit to become active again, you must start the new unit process which takes more time. If your charter is delinquent, your scouts cannot advance and your unit cannot function. Don't despair, help is available.

Contact the District Commissioner or the District Executive for a assistance.

Upcoming Training

Don't guess, get trained. You will get better results with less effort with training.

  • Backcountry Advanced Training (BAT) Classrooms sessions February 1 and February 8, 2023, 6:30-9PM at FUMC. Campout February 24-26, 2023, at TRJ Camp Meisenbach.
    Philmont bound? This is the training for both adults and scouts to prepare you for a high adventure trek. Registration and more information.
  • Introduction Leadership Skills for Troops (ILST) April 1-2, 2023 at Camp Wisdom.
    This training is for youth leaders in your unit and teaches them leadership skills. Registration and more information.
  • Basic Adult Leader Outdoor Orientation (BALOO) April 29-30, 2023 at Camp Wisdom
    This training is for Cub Scout Leaders and parents.
    This overnight activity equips you with the knowledge to take scouts on outdoor activities and ensures that you will have a safe and enjoyable experience for all participants. Registration coming soon.
  • Leader Specific for Scouts BSA leaders. Date & Location TBD
    Learn the basics of troop operation by experiencing troop operations as your scout does. Date, location and Registration coming soon.
  • Introduction to Outdoor Leader Skills (IOLS) April 29-30, 2023 at Camp Wisdom
    This training is for Scouts BSA leaders. This is the continuation of Leader specific training and adds an actual campout experience to complete your training. Learn the fundamentals of the outdoor program, letting your scouts lead while ensuring a safe and enjoyable activity. Registration coming soon.

Youth Protection training is only valid for two years. Every registered adult must have a current card. Don't wait until the last minute to complete is very necessary task. my.scouting.org

In addition to YPT every leader must be position trained. There are specific training programs for each of the many positions in scouting.

Beyond position training, Hazardous weather training is also required, so don't overlook this as well.

Wood Badge 140 - April 14-16 and 29-30, 2023 | Camp Wisdom

Wood Badge is the Boy Scouts of America's ultimate leadership training designed to meet the advanced leadership needs of Scouters in all aspects of the BSA, whether unit, district, or council level – from assistant den leaders to Scoutmasters, from Cubmasters to Venturing Advisors, from committee members to commissioners. It is a fun, energetic and inspiring course guaranteed to infuse your unit with fun and meaning – all designed to fulfill the mission of the BSA and ensure our youth are getting everything they are promised from the program.
