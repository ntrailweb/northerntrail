Twilight Camp Informational Online Meeeting
Wednesday April 29th 6:30 PM

We know you have questions about your Cub Scout’s day camp and while we do not have all of the answers, we want to share with you our plans to operate the 18 Cub Scout day camps taking place within the Circle Ten Council.

Join Circle Ten Council for a Zoom meeting on Wednesday April 29th beginning at 6:30 PM. There will have a presentation on what the council’s plans are for Cub Scout day and twilight camps and then have a question and answer session. This meeting is intended for Circle Ten Council parents of Cub Scouts and any volunteer leaders interested in our Cub Scout day and twilight camps.

Please register by April 28 and register only once per household as the council’s Zoom account has a capacity of 1,000 participants. You need to finish the registration process by going through the CHECK-OUT process. There is no fee, but the process needs to be completed for you to receive the Zoom meeting link/invitation. Finally, the link to the Zoom account will be sent out 24-48 hours prior to the meeting so it is important that you sign up HERE to ensure that you receive the link/invitation to this meeting.
