Online Camp Card Sale

To help support Scouting in Northern Texas/Southern Oklahoma during these uncertain times, the Circle Ten Council is taking the unprecedented step to offer its camp cards for sale online through the month of June.

During the registration process you will be asked to support a unit. If that unit is already signed up to sell camp cards, then that unit will receive its 50% commission (minus credit card fee and shipping). https://scoutingevent.com/571-37480-90360
