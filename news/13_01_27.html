Arapaho Chapter -- Congratulations to Chief Rory Costigan and the Arapaho Chapter Arrowmen! You have helped the Chapter to have another successful year and we have once again reached our goal of "Honor Chapter."

Mikanakawa News -- Mikanakawa News - Congratulations to Tim Conard who completed his 3 year role as OA Mikanakawa Lodge Adviser. Tim is a former Chapter Adviser for the Yanush Chapter which is now the Arapaho Chapter. The Governor's Trail bridge Tim was instrumental in getting built at Clements Scout Ranch was renamed in his honor. In addition Tim received the National William T. Hornaday Award recognizing his contribution to conservation.

Scouting for Food Signup / Bag Pickup -- The Scouting for Food bags have arrived. The bags can be picked up between 7:00 pm - 9:00 pm at Town North Presbyterian Church (1776 N. Plano Road, Richardson, Texas) on Monday 2/4. Otherwise, contact Dan Bruce at dbruce@sb-texas.com to arrange for a pickup of the bags. There are a few areas that have not been claimed. Please consider participating or taking an additional area, if possible. Contact Dan Bruce if you need to pickup your bags. The bags will be distributed on February, 2/9/13 and the food picked up on 2/16/13. If you have questions, please contact Dan Bruce at dbruce@sb-texas.com. The local food banks depend on the scouts to restock their shelves. More information.
