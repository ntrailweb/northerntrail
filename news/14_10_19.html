Circle Ten Council Advancement Academy Saturday, November 1, 2014

The Advancement Academy is a day-long adult training experience which will provide the participants with a thorough understanding of advancement in packs, troops and crews. The sessions will be facilitated by knowledgeable council and national trainers. They will cover a number of topics of general interest to all participants in the morning including the new edition of the Guide to Advancement and three breakout sessions in the afternoon will address topics concerning advancement specific to packs, troops or crews. More information

Webelos Woods 11/7-9/2014

Webelos Woods is district-organized event that culminates in a weekend of fun outdoor activities with the intent of providing opportunities for advancement and to aid with the Webelos-to-Scout transition. Webelos and their adult partner will get an interactive preview of the Boy Scouting program through a series of fun-filled activities accentuating the scouting experience. More information

Cub Scout Roundtable 11/13/2014 Thursday at 7:00PM

The primary objective of Cub Scout roundtable is to provide program ideas for your next den or pack meeting. You will see ideas demonstrated, and participate in the: games; songs; skits; and activities that are a part of every roundtable. You will meet other scouters in similar roles, share ideas, and learn from each other.

Friends of Scouting

Scouting depends on volunteers and contributions to make the program run. Please take a moment to schedule a time for a presenter to come to your Court of Honor or Blue and Gold Banquet and make a short presentation about Friends of Scouting. It will only take a about 15 minutes. It 's essential that our local Circle Ten Council have adequate funds to implement this great program with the necessary camps, professional staff and service centers. Without these assets the program could not function. Make your reservation here and someone will contact you to confirm.
