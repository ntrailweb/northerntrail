Camp Cards -- The Camp Card program is gearing up now; units should appoint a Camp Card Coordinator and decide how many cards they would like to order. The deadline for ordering cards is February 19 and the Council Camp Card Kickoff is March 4th at which time the unit's cards will be distributed. The best and easiest way to get signup up is for your unit's coordinator to go ONLINE and sign your unit up to sell cards. The second way is to contact the ADC assigned to your unit and work with him or her to get your unit signed up. Then have your Camp Card Coordinator attend the Kickoff on March 4 at the King of Glory King of Glory Lutheran Church, 6411 LBJ Freeway Dallas, TX 75240 MAP to pick up your cards.

Camporee -- March 23-24, 2013. Camp Trevor-Rees Jones. Units may now sign up and make campsite choices. Staff needed, please sign up today. Contact Tim Meeks at tim.meeks@ablsba.com if you have questions.

Re-Charter Deadline & Help -- For most units in Northern Trail, charters expire at the end of February. If your unit has not completed the re-charter process, now is the time to complete this task. Help is available. Wait too long and your charter will lapse. If that happens you are no longer a scout unit and you will have start from scratch. Don't let that happen.

Friends of Scouting Presentations -- Unit leaders, please take a moment and schedule a time for your unit's FOS presentation. On-line form. This will help tremendously in the process of scheduling volunteer FOS presenters. A court of honor or a Blue and Gold banquet is a good time. The presentation will only require 10 to 15 minutes during your event.

Face-to-Face Youth Protection Training -- Tuesday, February 26 at 7:00PM. Dartmouth Elementary. This training needed for adult participation in some events, particularly Twilight Camp and Summer Camp. Sign up today.

Oak Leaf Training -- Weekend of 4/6-7/2013 at Camp Wisdom. Your scouts will make better leaders with good leadership training. Registration now available. Make plans to attend.
