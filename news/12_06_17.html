A New Beginning:

This the very first Northern Trail District newsletter. As in the past, we are going to try and keep the website, www.ntrail.org synchronized with the newsletter, so if you see something in the newsletter you should be able to find the item on the website. To keep the newsletters short and to the point we will continue our practice of short descriptive headlines linked to more detail on an external website. Since this newsletter is directed at scouters involved in all levels of scouting from Tigers to Venturing, there will always be content which is not of general interest. The goal is for the reader to be able to quickly scan the descriptions and find the ones relevant to them. As always we are anxious to get feedback both good and bad. Of course, errors in content need to be corrected quickly, so please take a moment and report these to the webmaster@ntrail.org.

Be sure and remind everyone in your unit that they should sign up for and read the newsletter. The signup link is on the website and it just takes a minute to enter the required information.

Commissioner Service:

If you have been involved in scouts for a long time and particularly if your child has moved on, commissioner service is a great way to keep up with scouting and make a difference. A commissioner is an individual who is knowledgable about the program and has first hand knowledge of the challenges which face new leaders. The job of a commissioner is not to directly work with a unit, but rather be a mentor and sounding board for new leaders who are just getting started and need advice and encouragement. Commissioner service requires a minimal investment of time, but is very rewarding. If you think you might be interested in commissioner service, please contact our Distrct Commissioner, Dick Dawson at dick.dawson@sbcglobal.net.
