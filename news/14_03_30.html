Venturing Rendezvous 2014, Saturday April 12th

Shooting Sports, Archery, Mountain Biking, Tomahawk Throwing, Zip Line, Foundry and a growing list of others will be featured at Rendezvous, a Venturing program. Venturing is for all scouts - male and female - 14 years old and above. If you have an older girl who wants do what her brother has been doing - camping in tents, hiking, exploring the outdoors, mountain biking, zip lining, rock climbing, then come check out Rendezvous! Bring a friend and come Saturday, April 12 at Camp Wisdom. See www.circle10.org/venturing-rendezvous for more information and online registration.
