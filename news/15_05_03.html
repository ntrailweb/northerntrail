Twilight Camp is Coming Fast

Don't wait until the last minute to register for Twilight Camp. Please take the time to sign up today. We need to know how many are coming to plan and have enough supplies and volunteers to accommodate everyone. Avoid the rush and help our hardworking volunteers! Twilight camp begins on Monday June 8th. Tigers come on Wednesday June 10th. Twilight Camp is great fun for all participants parents and scouts alike. Walking den leaders needed. All walking den leaders must have a current within two years Face to Face Youth Projection card. Sign up today! Consider staffing! Adult and junior staff needed in almost every areas. Sign-up forms for participants and staff may be found on the webpage. ntrail.org/twilight

Youth Protection Training is Required

Face to Face YPT in May at First United Methodist Church. All leaders are required to have Youth Protection Training. Immediately following the Face to Face training you will have the opportunity to receive walking den leader training. The on-line version of youth protection training is available at myscouting.org, but the Face to Face version is required for Adult leaders at Twilight and Overnight Camps.

Mountaineer Weekends at the Summit

Mountaineer Weekends are the perfect way to get a feel for The Summit in a short period of time, whether passing through or just making a weekend of it.

Get a taste of the adventure and action sports opportunities available at the BSA’s newest national base — The Summit Bechtel Reserve — located in Southern West Virginia. The Mountaineer Weekend program gives you access to all venues in the Scott Summit Center. This area is a microcosm of the Summit with sample versions of each of the exciting activities found elsewhere on the property. Continue reading.

Trainer Edge, June 6, 2015

Trainer’s EDGE is the required train-the-trainer course for Wood Badge and NYLT staffs. The purpose of the Trainer’s EDGE course is to provide and help develop the platform skills of a trainer. Only practice can polish these skills, but this course focuses on training behaviors and resources while offering hands‐on experience in methods and media. Continue reading
