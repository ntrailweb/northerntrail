University of Scouting 1/3/2015

The University of Scouting is an adult leader's learning adventure! https://circle10.camp-master.com. Please note that you must "log onto" the camp master system to actually select classes, but you can pay without logging on. Be sure you do both, pay and select your classes. It is an action-packed, fun-filled single day of supplemental training where you choose the sessions you wish to take. Sessions are led by experienced volunteers and professionals who will help you enhance your ability to deliver a fun and exciting program to the Scouts. Several sessions are being taught by Northern Trail Scouters including Geocaching to Promote Scouting (GEN601, 2 hours, 8:00 AM). Course Catalog
