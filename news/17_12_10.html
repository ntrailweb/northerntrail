Combined Roundtables Tuesday December 12, 2017 FUMC

Come one, come all it’s a party. December’s Roundtable is a mixer of Cub Scouts, Boy Scouts, Sea Scouts, Venture Scouts and S.T.E.M Scouts. Everyone is invited. We’re going to meet, greet and possibly do some recruiting of Packs by Troops. Meet the leaders, and set up your Webelos visits. Recruiting season is about to open.
There will also be a visual history of Scouting through memorabilia collections. Your invited to bring your collection of patches, art works, vintage uniforms, photos and other things you may want to show. And, if you will, please bring a covered dish of your favorite dessert.
Your take away:

  • Meet Webelos parents looking for a Troop.
  • Meet Troop representatives and set up Troop visits.
  • Meet Venture Scout Crews, Sea Scouts Ships, and S.T.E.M Scouts representatives to learn who they are and what they do. They are all Co-ed Programs.
  • Meet other Scouters and share stories as well as ideas.
  • Enjoy seeing recent Scouting history through memorabilia.
Everyone is invited. Please bring Moms and Dads from your unit. Also, bring parents who have an interest in bringing their sons and daughters into Scouting.

Tuesday, December 12th 7:00 PM, Education building Room 230A

University of Scouting, Saturday, January 6, 2018

Are you a new leader of a troop, pack or den? If so, the University of Scouting is for you. Tons of great training on how to deliver a quality program without having to re-invent all the details. Trust me, others have trod the trail before you and they are anxious to pass on valuable knowledge. There is something for everyone, neophyte and veteran scouters alike.

The University of Scouting offers a wide range of unique adult leader training courses for Cub Scout Leaders, Boy Scout Leaders, Venturing Leaders, Varsity Leaders and Ship Leaders.

More information and course catalog circleten.org/university-scouting. Signup now available on CampMaster.

Philmont or High Adventure Bound?

This training is for both scouts and adults and is a comprehensive introduction or refresher for backpacking. Both classroom training and weekend backpacking trip. Two classes, 7:00PM Thursday February 15, 2018 and Wednesday February 21, 2018. Weekend campout, Weekend of March 2-4,2018. More information and registration.

Wood Badge

Reflecting the best of nearly a century of Scouting experience, the course draws upon the most current leadership models used by corporate America, academic circles, and successful outdoor organizations throughout the country. It builds on the best traditions and experiences of Wood Badge and also draws from a wide range of sources within and beyond the bounds of Scouting to present the latest in leadership theory and team development.
