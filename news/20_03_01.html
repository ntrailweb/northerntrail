National Youth Leadership Training March 15-20, Camp James Ray

Week long leadership training for scouts. Spring Break session. The NYLT course centers around the concepts of what a leader must BE, what he or she must KNOW, and what he or she must DO. The key elements are then taught with a clear focus on HOW TO. The skills come alive during the week as the patrol goes on a Quest for the Meaning of Leadership. Read more

Venturing Rendezvous March 20-22,2020

Venturing Rendezvous is a weekend of fun activities for co-ed youth ages 14 to 21. Come for the day on Saturday, March 20th or camp for the weekend, March 20-22.
