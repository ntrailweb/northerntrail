Do a Good Turn Daily - Scouting for Food February 10, 2018

Every year we leave areas uncovered in Northern Trail. These areas represent food that could have been placed in the hands of needy families. This happened because we failed to simply go out and get it. This year we can change that by covering those areas. If your unit has not signed up for an area, please do so today. If you have signed up for an area, please consider adopting an area which has not been spoken for. There are several which have not been covered in several years. Scoutmaster/Assistant Leader Specific Training, March 21, 28, 2018

Registration is now available. Boy Scout Leader Specific is a two evening course which will explain in detail how scouting works from meetings, patrols, boy leadership, safety and camping. This course is for all adults including parents, committee members and front line leaders. This class meets on two Wednesday evenings, March 21 and March 28th.

Introduction to Outdoor Leader Skills Training, April 7-8, 2018

This is the companion course for Scoutmaster/Assistant Leader Specific Training. This course builds on the material with a hands on actual campout where you experience a campout just as the scouts do. You will learn by doing at the direction of an experienced leadership team. Advance registration and preparation is required. The event will be held Saturday-Sunday April 7,8 at Camp Wisdom. More information and registration.

Circle Ten Council Annual Recognition Banquet - April 5, 2018

Congratulations to all the Northern Trail Scouts who earned their Eagle Scout rank in 2017! What a wonderful accomplishment! Parents, are you aware that Circle Ten Council honors all the previous year's Eagle Scouts at the annual banquet, and that their attendance is complimentary, for them and their Scout Master? When they receive their invitation in the mail, please RSVP and make plans to attend. They will be recognized with the whole John Stewart 2017 Class of Eagles, as well as be included in the group photo that will hang in the Murchison Scouting Center, and receive a special commemorative Council Strip Patch. Eagles must attend to receive the patch.

In addition, 24 deserving Scouters will be recognized with the Silver Beaver Award, as well as many others. Please join us as we celebrate and recognize the accomplishments of our Council! Follow this link to a flyer you can use to share with your units.
