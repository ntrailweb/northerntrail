PROGRAM

Eagle Scouts: Where Am I Now?

Panel discussion will feature several Eagle Scouts from Northern Trail District to discuss how Scouting has shaped their lives.

Bryan Wendell – Panel Moderator

Bryan Wendell, an Eagle Scout, is the senior editor of Boys’ Life, Scouting and Eagles’ Call magazines. He also writes the Bryan on Scouting blog, which shares news and advice for Scout leaders. Bryan grew up in Circle Ten, beginning as a Tiger Cub and continuing in Scouting until he turned 18. Along the way, he attended two national jamborees, two Philmont treks and multiple junior-leader training courses.

Bryan was a member of Troop 1776 in Plano. After high school, he received bachelor’s degrees in journalism and Spanish from Texas Tech University and a master’s in journalism from the University of Missouri. He worked at the Dallas Morning News before joining the Boy Scouts of America in 2008.
