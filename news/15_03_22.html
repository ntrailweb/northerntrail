Loyal

The second of the 12 points of the Scout Law is "Loyal." A Scout is true to his family, friends, Scout leaders, school, and nation. Scouting reinforces this basic value.

Re-Charter Past Due?

Some units in Northern Trail are within 1 week of losing their charter? If your unit is among these, take action. Unit commissioners and the district executive are available to assist you with the process. Even if you are not the unit chairman or leader, getting the re-charter done is everyone's job. If your leaders have been asking for paperwork or needed youth protection training, please help them out by completing these tasks. More Information

Activities For Your Scouts

There are a number of upcoming activities and it's time to get signed up. These include: Scouting University (Merit Badges) and Oak Leaf (Leadership Training) for Boy Scouts. Venturing Rendezvous Weekend for Venturing Age Youth. Twilight Camp for Cub Scouts. Parent -N- Pal Weekend for Cubscouts. Webelos Resident Camp for Webelos Scouts. All of these activities require advanced sign up. Don't make the job of the organizers more difficult than it needs to be. Sign up promptly.

Training for Parents and Leaders

Overwhelmed by the program? Do you want your child to stay in scouts? Having trouble getting other parents to help? We have a solution to these and other things you have not even thought about. Training! Scout training is first rate and it's fun. So don't put it off, sign up and get up to speed on the program. Every registered adult must have youth protection training and some activities like Twilight Camp and Summer Camp require the Face to Face version of this training. Outdoor Leader Skills Training, Face to Face Youth protection and Wood Badge now available. Check the www.ntrail.org website for information about training opportunities.
