Fall Activities

Summer vacation will quickly pass and the new school year will be upon us. With the new school year comes, Join Scouting Night and Popcorn. If you have not identified leads for these two important events, please do so as soon as possible.

Keep in mind that in the Fall it will be necessary to get your Unit Re-chartered. This process can be simple or complicated. If you start early, it's not at all difficult. Wait until the last minute and it can be a real burden for everyone. Resist the temptation to put this off! Here is a handy checklist which will get you started! http://northerntrail.circleten.org/recharter-checklist. Remember leaders must be trained for their respective positions and all registered adults must have up-to-date youth protection training.

If you are not responsible for the re-charter, you can still help, get your required training done and pay any dues on time!

National Youth Leadership Training (NYLT)

National Youth Leadership Training is an intense, 5 ½ day, outdoor leadership training experience for youth troop, team and crew leaders. The course goal is to prepare young men and women to become superior leaders in their troop, crew, district, council, and other organizations they belong to now, and in the future.   Continue reading
