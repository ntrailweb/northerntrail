Popcorn

Does your unit need to sign-up for the 2020 popcorn sale? Click here for more popcorn reward information. For more information please contact Rachel Joslyn at Rachel.Joslyn@scouting.org.

COVID-19 has put a significant financial strain on the council. Popcorn sales is a big part of the income stream for the council and it helps units raise money for their programs and scout accounts. Recognize Adult Scouters

Scouting only exists due the tireless work of volunteers and the unit, district, council and national level. We are indebted to these individuals who are critical to the success of the scouting movement.

We have a number of awards to recognize these individuals begining at the unit level with the Your Extraordinary Service Award or Y.E.S. At the district level we have two awards the District Award of Merit and the Robert E. Little Award. Applications for these awards must be submitted to Dr. Harry Yankuner hy18@sbcglobal.net no later than December 12th. Applications will not be accepted after this date, no exceptions.

At the Council level we have the Silver Beaver Award. Applications for the Silver Beaver Award must be received by Dr. Harry Yankuner hy18@sbcglobal.net no later than November 19th. Applications will not be accepted after this date, no exceptions.

Self nominations will not be accepted.
