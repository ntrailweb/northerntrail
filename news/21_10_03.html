Training for Scouts (Registration is closed)

ILST is Introduction to Leadership Skills for Troops. October 8-10 at Camp Wisdom. This is for any Scouts BSA Patrol Leader Council member. Course has replaced Oak Leaf. Teaches troop organization, leadership skills, how to teach using EDGE. The course is designed to teach by using games and other methods to engage youth. Scout's Own is included in the course to be a guide to scouts who serve as Chaplain's aide. Reference material for Scouts is the Patrol Leader's Handbook. On-line Registration. It is Time to Recognize Adult Scouters

Scouting only exists due the tireless work of volunteers and the unit, district, council and national level. We are indebted to these individuals who are critical to the success of the scouting movement.

We have several awards to recognize these individuals beginning at the unit level with the Your Extraordinary Service Award or the Y.E.S. Award. At the district level we have two awards: the District Award of Merit and the Robert E. Little Award. Applications for these awards must be submitted no later than Friday, December 10th. No applications will not be accepted after this date.

At the Council level we have the Silver Beaver Award. Applications for the Silver Beaver Award must be received no later than Friday, November 12th. No applications will not be accepted after this date.

Self-nominations will not be accepted.

More information, including the submission details of these awards, is available here.

For any questions, please contact the Awards and Recognition Chair, Muhammad Ikram at mzi@gatech.edu.
