Adult Award Nomination Deadline 12/10/2021

Time is running out.  In January, the district will hold the annual awards banquet where we will recognize leaders in our district who have demonstrated outstanding leadership through their contributions to scouting. 

It only takes a few minutes to nominate someone, consider the many hours of effort these volunteers have put into the program. Please take the time to nominate them.

  • At the unit level, we have the Your Extraordinary Service Award, commonly called the YES Award. Every Unit receives 2 nominations, units that have 50-99 youth members are entitled to an additional nomination, and Units with 100 or more youth members are allowed a total of 4 nominations. Submission deadline is 12/10/21.  We have an online nomination form which may be found here: ntrail.org/yes. It's a simple process to fill out the form on-line and make the submission. 
  • At the district level, we have two awards: the District Award of Merit (DAM) and the Robert E. Little Award. Nomination submission deadline for these awards is 12/10/21.   Forms and more information about these two awards may be found here:  https://ntrail.org/Scouts/northerntrail.circleten.org/adult-recognition-0.html
