Re-Charter

We are down to the wire, get it done. Don't let the lack of current YPT cards trip up your re-charter, make sure everyone in your unit is current.

Help is available, all you have to do is ask.

Every unit’s charter expires on December 31. The recharter process is simpler than it has been in the past and by uploading documents and paying online, you can avoid a trip to a council office. The process even allows for the gathering of required signatures online. Let’s get started now and be 100% rechartered on January 1. For more help, contact you Unit Commissioner.

If you are not responsible for the re-charter process you are still responsible to get your training up to date and any dues paid. Youth Protection Training is required for all adults and must be completed every two years.

Any questions or problems please contact your unit commissioner or Steve Bloberger who can be reached at: steve_bloberger@icloud.com

Religious Emblems

Make earning religious emblems part of your program. Just about every faith tradition has a religious emblem. The religious emblems program is recognized by BSA as a way for Scouts in all age groups to develop a deeper understanding of their faith. To get started, speak with your spiritual advisor or visit https://www.scouting.org/awards/religious-awards/.
