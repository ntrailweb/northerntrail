Scouting for Food -- Coming in February, 2/9/13 Bags out and 2/16/13 Food pickup. Units that wish to participate in Scouting for Food in February, please have an appropriate representative for the Unit contact Dan Bruce at dbruce@sb-texas.com and indicate your unit's desire to participate. With the holidays, the pantry shelves will be empty and they depend on the scouts to help restock.
