Adult Recognition

Time is running out, please take a moment to recognize leaders in your units.

On January 23 at the District Awards Banquet, we will recognize volunteers who have made the extra effort to make scouting possible in our units and the district.

Every unit should have at least one nominee for a Y.E.S. award. Being recognized for one's efforts is appreciated and volunteers are more likely to continue to contribute if they feel their efforts are making a difference.  Then plan to bring them to the dinner and help us recognize their contributions.  

The District Awards of Merit and the Robert E. Little Award recognize district level volunteers who make a profound impact on the program delivered at the district level.

Deadlines for Y.E.S.,   R.E.L.   and D.A.M have been extended. More information and nomination forms may be found here. Questions to: Dr. Harry Yankuner HY18@sbcglobal.net

University of Scouting Saturday January 4, 2020

A reminder that University of Scouting 2020 online registration closes Dec. 22.

Register now for Circle Ten's largest one-day training event. Come enjoy a day of Scouting fellowship and build your knowledge to make your units better by attending University of Scouting 2020.

Adult leaders from the newest parent to the most experienced Scouter will find something to learn at Scouting Vision 2020-Looking to the Future. There also are two courses available for qualified youth, The Venturing Academy and The SPL Leadership Academy.

Re-charter Time

Don't let your charter drop. Help is available.

If you are not responsible for the actual re-charter process, you are responsible for getting your membership dues paid and completing the necesssary forms and have a valid Youth Protection Card. These expire every two years, so for many people this is a task which must be completed now.

If you have any questions, please contact your unit commissioner or
