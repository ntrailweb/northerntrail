Obedient

From the Scout Handbook - "Obedient. Follow the rules of your family, school, and pack. Obey the laws of your community and country." It can be difficult for children to understand why we have rules, but agreed upon rules, make our families and communities function. Leaders, encourage your scouts to be Obedient.

National Youth Leadership Training

National Youth Leadership Training is an exciting, action-packed program designed for councils to provide youth members with leadership skills and experience they can use in their home troops and in other situations demanding leadership of self and others.

The NYLT course centers around the concepts of what a leader must BE, what he must KNOW, and what he must DO. The key elements are then taught with a clear focus on HOW TO. The skills come alive during the week as the patrol goes on a Quest for the Meaning of Leadership. Learn more.

Dive St Croix

Let's Dive St Croix, USVI! Dallas Crew 890 has 4 spots available for this summer's scuba trip to St. Croix, US Virgin Islands, July 19-25.

Participants must already be scuba certified, 14 years or older and meet health criteria. Trip is open to males (Adult Male needed) and females. Cost is $1,290 per person, plus airfare and spending money. This is Sea Base's last year to offer scuba in St. Croix; going forward scuba will only be offered in the Florida Keys.
