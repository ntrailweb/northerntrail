Opportunities In Advanced Adult Leadership Training

Lord Baden Powell once said every youth deserves a trained leader. He also said training is the most important responsibility of the unit leader. As a consequence, Circle 10 Council, including Northern Trail District, has a rich history in leadership training. It directly impacts the quality of the program delivered in the units and districts, and it has been shown to be reflected in youth retention. What does that mean? It means the youth are enjoying scouting and are staying involved. It means adults understand their roles and how to achieve their goals through team work and leadership. In other words, no one expects you to do it all, and this is how you learn to get others involved. That is why you have seen so many people talk about Wood Badge training.
