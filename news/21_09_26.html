Northern Trail to Adventure, October 9

Northern Trail to Adventure is an event for all Cub Scout age youth to come enjoy the hands-on experiences and outdoor fun of Cub Scouting. The event will be held at Camp Wisdom, Saturday October 9. Map

Check-in will be at 8:30 AM, with activities concluding around 1:30 PM. During the event ALL Cub Scouts, friends, and siblings will be able to participate in Crafts, Archery, BB Gun Shooting, and Games at the Pirate Ships! Registration available here.

Parent-N-Pal at Trevor Rees-Jones October 8th-10th

All registered Cub Scouts and their parents/guardians are invited to experience a fun-filled weekend! Activities include archery, BB guns, horseback riding, games and more! All you must do is bring your tent, sleeping bag, personal gear, flashlight and camp chair. All activities and food are provided! Cost is $60 per person, if paid by September 23rd. Register online.

Training for Scouts (Registration closes tonight, Sunday Sept 26.)

ILST is Introduction to Leadership Skills for Troops. October 8-10 at Camp Wisdom. This is for any Scouts BSA Patrol Leader Council member. Course has replaced Oak Leaf. Teaches troop organization, leadership skills, how to teach using EDGE. The course is designed to teach by using games and other methods to engage youth. Scout's Own is included in the course to be a guide to scouts who serve as Chaplain's aide. Reference material for Scouts is the Patrol Leader's Handbook. On-line Registration. It is Time to Recognize Adult Scouters

Scouting only exists due the tireless work of volunteers and the unit, district, council and national level. We are indebted to these individuals who are critical to the success of the scouting movement.

We have a number of awards to recognize these individuals beginning at the unit level with the Your Extraordinary Service Award or the Y.E.S. Award. At the district level we have two awards; the District Award of Merit and the Robert E. Little Award. Applications for these awards must be submitted no later than Friday, December 10th. No applications will not be accepted after this date.

At the Council level we have the Silver Beaver Award. Applications for the Silver Beaver Award must be received no later than Friday, November 12th. No applications will not be accepted after this date.

Self nominations will not be accepted.

More information, including the submission details of these awards, is available here.

For any questions, please contact the Awards and Recognition Chair, Muhammad Ikram at mzi@gatech.edu.
