Recruiting New Scouts / School Night for Scouting

Materials to help you are available at https://circleten.ihubapp.org/posts/55347/2020-recruitment-resources. There is an order form link on that page where you can order Cub Scout Business Cards, Scouts BSA Business Cards, Door Hangers (Cub Specific), Sticky Notes (Cub Specific), Yard Sign for families (Cub only), Yard Sign for the community (Generic Join Scouting), and customized videos which will include things such as your unit, a contact with phone and/or email, a date for a meeting, etc. Every Scouting family should have a yard sign, either Cub Specific or generic. Popcorn

Does your unit need to sign-up for the 2020 popcorn sale? Here are some incentives, if a Scout sells $250 dollars in popcorn they can attend day camp/winter camp for free or $100 dollars off an in-council camp. Click here for more popcorn reward information. For more information please contact Rachel Joslyn at Rachel.Joslyn@scouting.org.

COVID-19 has put a significant financial strain on the council. Popcorn sales is a big part of the income stream for the council and it helps units raise money for their programs and scout accounts. The popcorn program has adapted to the new normal and there are innovative ways to sell. Check out the details and get signed up today.
