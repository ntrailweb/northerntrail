Adult Recognition - Call for nominations

Every year in January, the district holds a dinner to honor, not the scouts, but the countless adult volunteers who deliver the program. Volunteers work at every level of the scout program. You see the volunteers in your unit, but there are additional volunteers who serve at the district and the council level.  There is someone in your unit who should be nominated and recognized for their efforts. More information Deadline for Silver Beaver nominations is November 1, 2012. All other award nominations must be submitted by December 1, 2012.
