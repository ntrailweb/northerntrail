2024 Friends of Scouting Campaign

It’s kick-off time for our annual FOS campaign! This year Northern Trail’s goal is to raise $178,000 of Circle Ten Council’s $4.2M total budget. Your unit will be visited this spring by one of our FOS presenters, who’ll provide information about this vital fundraising program. We hope you’ll help us communicate to your units the importance of FOS giving.

Financial supporters will receive thank you gifts, including:

    $300 - $499 – a Scout sticker and awesome hat.
    $500 and above – a Scout sticker, hat and 2024 shoulder patch.

Note: If a $500+ donation is received by mid-February, the donor will also have a seat at the February 29 FOS dinner featuring Michael Pompeo, former CIA Secretary and Secretary of State.

Thanks in advance for your support!

Northern Trail District Dinner, Feb 22, 2024, FUMC

Celebrating 2023 Eagle Scouts and Adult Volunteers. Flier. Everyone must register. 2023 Eagle Scouts - free until 2/12/2023. Adults/Youth - $40. Early Bird Registration ends 2/12/2024. Registration closes 2/19/2024. No walk-ins.

Our keynote speaker will be Darin Kinder. Darin Kinder is a special agent with the United States Secret Service. On September 11, 2001, he was in the heart of New York City when the World Trade Centers were attacked. His heroic actions earned him the Secret Service Medal of Valor.

Arapaho Chapter News

It's time to register your unit for Order of the Arrow Elections. Units are encouraged to sign up as soon as possible to reserve their date and time. The deadline for all elections this season is March 30th.

Contact Kevin Smith arapaho@miki.org to request an election date.

The Order of the Arrow is an honor camper service organization. Each district has a chapter and members are chosen by their peers.

Commissioners

Northern Trail has an active commissioner staff with deep scouting knowledge. Get to know your commissioner. Your commissioner can help you and your unit resolve problems and pass along some ideas to make your program even better. Our District Commissioner is Bram Franklin, who may be reached at: bramfranklin@gmail.com.

If you are having trouble reaching your commissioner, contact Bram and he will assist you.

Commissioner Service

Have your scouts aged out of scouting? Are you looking to apply your scouting skills? Commissioner service is a way to continue contributing to scouting by mentoring unit leaders, hosting roundtables and helping staff events. Training is available! Contact our district commissioner Bram Franklin bramfranklin@gmail.com for more information.

Membership + Recruiting

  • Follow up on New Members: Pack Unit Leaders, please make note of newly recruited members who you have not seen recently. A simple phone call, email or even a postcard can make the difference. Remember, you understand the value of the scout program, with new parents you must sell the program.
  • Grow your Membership: Growing your membership is a year-round activity. You should always be looking for opportunities to recruit more scouts into the program! If you need assistance with recruiting new members contact Katherine McCrary at katherine.mccrary@scouting.org for suggestions.
