Twilight Camp, June 3-7, 2019

Dear Cub Scout Leaders and Parents,
Twilight Camp is 7 weeks away! More important regular registration ends in 2 weeks! In the past you have been able to sign up for camp before the deadline to save your spots and pay after Camp Card sales ended. This year, with the change in the registration system that option is not available and the full fee must be paid by April 30 to avoid paying the late fee. This is not a Northern Trail decision and we regret this change.

Next, the new system has another problem related to the one discussed above. When a registration is made the new system will not save your registrations without some payment being made. To avoid this, please try to make a full payment. If you or your unit is unable to make a full payment, please make a partial payment ($5.00, for example) and pay the balance later-but remember that the full fee must be made by April 30 to avoid the late fee.

Finally, Twilight Camp needs staff, especially for BB guns and archery. There is Rangemaster training for both Archery and BB guns on May 4 and everyone thinking of working or assisting on the ranges should take this training. To assist contact, Hank Voegtle at 214-769-5040 or . Also, register for staff following the link below.
