National Council News

As was reported on Monday, the National BSA filed Chapter 11 Bankruptcy. You can help counteract all the negative publicity and misinformation by sharing the following items with your units. Here is a sharable graphic which describes the details of what is happening. More detailed information may be found here.

Remember that this bankruptcy will not affect us locally as members of Circle Ten Council. We remain as strong and dedicated as always to deliver this great program to our youth. It is more important than ever that everyone support our council through Friends of Scouting now to keep us that way.

Venturing Rendezvous March 20-22,2020

Venturing Rendezvous is a weekend of fun activities for co-ed youth ages 14 to 21. Come for the day on Saturday, March 20th or camp for the weekend, March 20-22. Located at Circle Ten Council's Camp Wisdom, 6400 W. Redbird Lane, Dallas, TX Cost: $40 per youth and $25 per adult attending if registered and paid by 11:59 p.m. Sunday, March 8th. $50 if registering between 12:00 a.m. Monday, March 9th and 11:59 p.m. Wednesday, March 18th. Online registration will close at midnight on March 18, 2020. Those who want to attend, but have not paid by this time may pay at the door $50. One T-Shirts per paid participant is included in the price. Everyone registered and paid by this date will receive a T-Shirt and a Patch. Be sure to visit the Add-On Products to give us a T-Shirt size for all paid participants, youth and adults. Extra T-Shirts can also be purchased for $12 each. Read more.
