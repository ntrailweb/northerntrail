Northern Trail

Friday June 3, 2022

Northern Trail Scout Family

June 7th at 6:00pm please join us for the annual Adult Recognition Banquet.  We will be holding this event during dinner time at Twilight Camp where many of you will already be volunteering, we will work quickly to get you in and out.  For those of you who are not volunteering at Twilight Camp please come on out and enjoy some BBQ and see some of your fellow scouters acknowledged for their contribution to the Northern Trail District.

We are hosting in a “potluck” style.  Steve Bloberger and I will be bringing the meats and we need side dishes to feed the masses.  John and Phyllis Slaughter will be bringing plates, cutlery, and cups.  We will have a great time but need YOU to be there to make it memorable.  We have District Award of Merit, Robert E. Little, Silver Beaver, and Y.E.S. (Your Extraordinary Service) recipients who will all be recognized.

Please come and join us at Breckenridge Park - entrance B - 3555 Brand Rd, Richardson, TX 75082 at 5:50pm so we can start at 6:00 sharp.  We look forward to seeing you there!

Thanks for all YOU do for Scouting,

Tom Crain
District Chairman
Northern Trail District
