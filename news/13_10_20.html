Too Much of a Boy Scout

Recently I visited the Gerald R. Ford Presidential Library. Prominently on display were his scout uniform, sash and his Eagle Badge. At one point someone said that he was too much of a boy scout to be president. He addressed this issue in a speech given at the National Awards Banquet in 1974. Read More.
