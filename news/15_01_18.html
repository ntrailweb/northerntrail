Arapaho Honored at Lodge Annual Dinner

At the Mikanakawa Lodge Annual Awards dinner, the Arapaho chapter took home several awards. The Chapter received Gold Level in Chapter Journey to Excellence Program. Several Arrowmen were recognized, individually, as well. Steve Healy was awarded a Silver Friendship Knot for Outstanding Service as an Adult Arrowman. Lastly, John Russell Niederer, Chris Miller, and Tick Hayes, were all called out for Vigil.

Fireside Chats

Here is an opportunity to meet with the Council President, Commissioner and Scout Executive at one of our five upcoming fireside chats.
