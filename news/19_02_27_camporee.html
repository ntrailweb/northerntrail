If you have ever been to a Camporee, then you know that it’s a lot of fun with scout skills competition, activities and an awesome campfire program.

Camporees are always high energy and fun regardless of the weather. We will however have great weather this year! Here are some pictures from a past camporee.

Camporee will be held at Camp Constantin/Jack D. Furst Aquatic Base which is located on Possum Kingdom Lake in Palo Pinto County, near Graford, Texas. Map. This is a great facility with tremendous views of the lake and spectacular local geology.

The theme this year will be Civil War East vs. West, expect some great and fun competitions based on the theme.

Making a return to Camporee are Troop First Aid Kit Inspections, please have your kit up to date and stocked for Camporee and have your troop’s scoutmaster or adult leader bring it to the Med Lodge where it will be inspected and scored for points.

Registration is now available on-line at http://northerntrail.circleten.org/camporee-march-22-24-2019.

Register and pay on or before March 19th and the cost for scouts is $15 and adults are $10. After March 19th the price will increase to $20 both scouts and adults.

Camporee staff, please register on-line as soon as possible and attend the planning sessions.

The leaders guide may be found here. If you have any questions please email camporee@ntrail.org
