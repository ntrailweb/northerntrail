Scouting For Food

We have not had many units sign up for Scouting for Food. This is a vitally important activity and our participation reflects a positive view of the scout movement. "A scout is helpful." Please get your unit signed up today and live the scout law!

Northern Trail District invites your unit to be part of the most visible activity of BSA, Scouting for Food.

Food insecurity continues to be a prevalent issue in our community, especially for children. 20% of children under the age of 18 are food insecure in Dallas County. "Food insecurity is defined by the USDA as the lack of access, at times, to enough food for an active, healthy life. Key drivers of food insecurity include unemployment, poverty, and income shocks, which can prevent adequate access to food." We can support The Network of Community Ministries in their mission to help the hungry in our area. One of the aims of Scouting is Character. Showing care for your fellow neighbor is a great way to display your Citizenship in the Community.
