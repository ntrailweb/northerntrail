A Day of Training -- Saturday 9/29/2012 More Information

This will be a day of training, the exact schedule is being finalized. It will be held at the First United Methodist Church of Richardson Map Offerings include:

  • Face to Face Youth Protection: This is the state mandated version required for adult leaders attending summer camp, winter camp and Twilight Camp.
  • Cub Scout Leader Specific:
    • Cubmaster
    • Pack Committee
    • Tiger Den Leader
    • Wolf/Bear Den Leader
    • Webelos Den Leader
  • Troop Committee Specific Training
  • Merit Badge/Life to Eagle
  • Den Chief (Training for youth wishing to serve as a den chief.)

Online registration for a day of Training will be available soon.
