It is Time to Recognize Adult Scouters

It is this time of year when we recognize our fellow scouters for the exemplary volunteer work that they do at the unit, district, and council levels. We remain thankful to them for their tireless service.

We have several awards to recognize these individuals beginning at the unit level with the Your Extraordinary Service Award or the Y.E.S. Award. At the district level we have two awards: the District Award of Merit and the Robert E. Little Award. Applications for these awards must be submitted no later than Friday, December 9th. No applications will be accepted after this date.

At the Council level we have the Silver Beaver Award. Applications for the Silver Beaver Award must be received no later than Monday, November 21th. No applications will be accepted after this date.

Self-nominations will not be accepted.

More information, including the submission details of these awards, is available here.

For any questions, please contact the Awards and Recognition Chair, Muhammad Ikram at mzi@gatech.edu.

Training

Youth Protection training is only valid for two years. Every registered adult must have a current card. Don't wait until the last minute to complete is very necessary task. my.scouting.org

In addition to YPT every leader must be position trained. There are specific training programs for each of the many positions in scouting.

Beyond position training, Hazardous weather training is also required, so don't overlook this as well.

We have in-person training opportunities coming including Adult Leader Training (ALT) for scouts BSA, Introduction to Outdoor Leader Skills IOLS for Scouts BSA and Basic Adult Leader Outdoor Orientation for Cub Scouts and Webelos.

Watch the calendar and newsletter for information about available training.

Introduction to Leadership Skills for Troops - Nov 5-6 Camp Wisdom

What: The purpose of the ILST (Introduction to Leadership Skills for Troops) course is to teach Scouts with leadership positions about their new roles and how to most effectively reach success in that role. ILST is the first course in the series of leadership training offered to Scouts and is a replacement for Troop Leadership Training. The next course in the series is National Youth Leadership Training.

Who: Youth desiring to be trained in the Leadership and Outdoor Skills required for their positions - Scouts BSA, boys and girls. The minimum age to participate is aged 12 at time of the course. Participants must be First Class and above. When: Arrive at 8:00 am on Saturday, November 5, 2022. Dismissal at 12:00 pm on Sunday, November 6, 2022. Staff arrive on Friday. Staff Needed: Both adult and scout staff needed.

Questions: Contact Bill Wilson wrw@billwilsonlaw.com if you have questions. Sign-up for ILST here.

Service Opportunity

Greetings Scouters. Do you have Scouts or a unit that needs Service hours or a project ..or just wants to 'do a good turn' and be 'helpful'?

The Arlington Charities organization (link), a non-profit charitable organization that fights 'hunger, poverty and homelessness' has asked for help from Scouts. The Program Director is Casey McCollum, a legacy Eagle Scout from North Trail District. He has started a program asking for donations of usable sleeping bags to be distributed to those folks on the streets with no shelter and in real danger in the coming cold months.

If you and your Scouts are willing to help, please check your unit storage rooms, the trailer and even theirs and your own Scout storage closet for older, no longer used, or extra sleeping bags that could be a life saver for someone in need.

If you find such sleeping bags, please contact John McCollum at 214.497.5276 or jmccollum0@gmail.com and I will arrange for collection and transport to the Arlington shelter.

Annual Re-Charter

Every unit must re-charter their unit each year. This involves several steps. You can start working on this now.

  • Update rosters both youth and adult.
  • Review training status for every adult. Current Youth Protection is required.
  • Collect adult and scout applications if required.
  • Collect fees

Start now and this process will be much easier. Wait until the last minute and it is hard on everyone.
