Camp Card Sales

This is a really easy way for scouts to earn their way to Camp, Philmont, other activities or simply support unit program or supply needs. Each Camp Card will sell for only $5.00 to the general public and will have multiple food, service and entertainment discounts for the customer to use throughout the year. The participating unit will keep 50% commission for each Camp Card sold! Click here for more information on Camp Card Sales.

Boy Scout Leader Specific, Begins 3/18/2015

This course for adult leaders and parents in Boy Scout units. The course is open to all parents and leaders and is designed for leaders who work directly with boys. The class meets 3 times, March 18, 25 and April 1. This a great way for a parent to become familiar with how a Boy Scout Troop operates.
