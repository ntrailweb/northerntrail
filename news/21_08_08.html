Training for Adults

Adult Leader specific training will be offered in one session on Saturday October 16th at the John D. Murchison Scouting Center 8605 Harry Hines Blvd. Dallas, TX 75235. This course is designed for Scouts BSA leaders and parents. You will learn how a scout unit functions by participating as a scout would.

Introduction to Outdoor Leader Skills training for Scouts BSA leaders will occur the weekend of October 23-24, 2021 at Camp Wisdom. 7:30am Saturday through 12:00pm on Sunday.

BALOO, Basic Adult Leader Outdoor Orientation for Cub Scout and Webelows leaders will be held the weekend of October 23-24 at Camp Wisdom. 7:30am Saturday through 12:00pm on Sunday.

Cub Scout Leader Specific Training. Course will be October 9 at Camp Wisdom, 9am-1pm. There are 3 modules: CubMaster/Asst Cub Master, Den Leader/Asst Den Leader, Committee Chair)

Training for Scouts

ILST is Introduction to Leadership Skills for Troops. October 8-10 at Camp Wisdom. This is for any Scouts BSA Patrol Leader Council member. Course has replaced Oak Leaf. Teaches troop organization, leadership skills, how to teach using EDGE. The course is designed to teach by using games and other methods to engage youth. Scout's Own is included in the course to be a guide to scouts who serve as Chaplain's aide. Reference material for Scouts is the Patrol Leader's Handbook. Registration coming soon.
