Emergency Risk Management

Now is a good time if you have not done it already to clean out your trailer and/or storage facility. For most of us with COVID-19 we have not used these. Bugs (Brown Recluse Spiders) can get into your items especially with the hot weather. While you are cleaning out check your first aid kits for completeness, expired items and items that might not be good if exposed to the heat. Check fire extinguishers to make sure they filled and not expired (date on bottom of the extinguisher) should be good for ten years. Finally, AED equipment check for expired pads and leads, is the battery charged properly, is there an adult responsible for the device and are the adults trained in CPR-BLS and is the certificate current. Questions: contact Risk Management Chair, Dr. Donald Blum donald.blum@yahoo.com.

Wood Badge Courses

Get signed up today to attend a Wood Badge Course!
Wood Badge is Boy Scouts of America's ultimate leadership training for adults; designed for leaders of all BSA programs to help show those leaders how to ensure their youth get the most out of the Scouting program. Wood Badge consists of two parts. The first is a five-day practical program, usually offered in two 3-day camping experiences. The second part, completed over the following 18 months, requires participants to apply the skills they have learned in their local Scouting program by completing five goals (tickets) leading towards the participant's vision.
