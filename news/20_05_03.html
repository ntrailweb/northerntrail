COVID-19 Response

While we are not out of the woods, the numbers continue to improve. Businesses are starting to reopen under strict guidelines. The council offices will be open beginning tomorrow with limited hours and operations. The scout shop is expected to open May 11th.

Twilight Camp

Twilight Camp 2020: Restructured

For 2020 Circle Ten Council has restructured Day and Twilight Camps council-wide because of coronavirus/COVID19. Because of that, Northern Trail District will not be holding our traditional Twilight Camp at Breckinridge Park.

Instead, there will be a council-wide an at home Day Camp program that begins on May 15. There will be activities posted on that date that Cubs and their families can do at home. Read more
