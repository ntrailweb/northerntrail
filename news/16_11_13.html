Chris Miller (1971-2016)

We are sad to report the passing of Vigil Brother Chris Miller, after a lengthy battle with diabetes and its complications. Chris was a long time member of our Chapter serving as an Associate Adviser for 5 years. As a youth, Chris had been the Chapter Chief of Yanush (before it became Arapaho). Chris could be found at many of our chapter meetings, district events and lodge functions, helping behind the scenes, as a model of Cheerful Service. He will be sorely missed. More information

Unit Inventory

Each year we do membership inventories. We request the units send their unit rosters of active youth so that we can check them against the official rosters at the council. This is helpful in finding boys that the unit believes to be registered, but they are not. Please ask that each unit send their rosters to Chad Caudle at Chad.caudle@scouting.org by Nov 18th. Get Started on your Re-Charter Today

Every unit must re-charter each year. On time re-charters are much easier than dealing with an expired charter. Start early and it's not overwhelming, wait until the last minute and it's very hard on everyone! Help is available, your unit commissioner can assist with the process. If you are not directly responsible for the re-charter process you can help. Every adult must have a current youth protection training card. Everyone needs to pay their membership fees.
