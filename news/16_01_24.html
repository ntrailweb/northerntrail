2015 Outstanding Eagle Project Award

Brian Daugherty, Troop 1001, received the first award for Outstanding Eagle Project of the Year at the District Dinner on January 21. Brian's project has also been nominated for the council's Outstanding Eagle Project of 2015 recognition.

Brian planned a multi-weekend effort and directed 23 scouts and adults in renovating the Youth Center at St. Joseph's Church in Richardson. His team build a set of bookshelves with a large cut-out cross in the center; repaired a large broken cross and placed it in the cut-out; and added writable walls to the area. This project took over 400 hours.

Brian did competitive shopping to find the best prices for materials, wrote a well-organized and detailed plan, and intentionally used the EDGE method to train his workers. He had to make several adjustments during his project, including how to fasten the shelves to the unexpected aluminum studs in the wall instead of the expected wooden studs.

His parents are Brian & Denise Daugherty.
