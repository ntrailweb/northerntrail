Re-charter Time

If you have completed your unit re-charter, Thank You! If not it's time to Get 'er Done! As of December 31st your unit will become "inactive" and scouts can't advance and your unit can't use the council facilities. Please take the time to complete the needed paperwork.

These units have submitted their recharter (as of 12/26/19): Crew 719, Crew 890, Pack 435, Pack 516, Pack 570, Pack 719, Pack 728, Pack 890, Pack 893, Pack 894, Pack 1077, Pack 2060, Troop 81, Troop 86, Troop 435, Troop 444B, Troop 444G, Troop 570, Troop 707, Troop 719, Troop 728B, Troop 728G, Troop 751, Troop 777, Troop 890B, Troop 890G, Troop 1001, Troop 1077, Troop 2054, and Troop 2060. That is only 30 units. There are 8 units that have begun the recharter. This means there are 15 units that have not started the online recharter process and you have only two days to complete the process.

Adult leaders, you must have a current youth protection card for your unit to complete your registration. These expire every two years. Take the online course at my.scouting.org/

Doing the paperwork for your unit? Help is available. Contact your unit commissioner or Hank Voegtle HJV3@outlook.com. More information may be found here.

University of Scouting Saturday January 4, 2020

The University of Scouting 2020 online registration closed on December. 28. If you did not register, walk-ins are available.

This is Circle Ten's largest one-day training event. Come enjoy a day of Scouting fellowship and build your knowledge to make your units better by attending University of Scouting 2020.

Adult leaders from the newest parent to the most experienced Scouter will find something to learn at Scouting Vision 2020-Looking to the Future. There also are two courses available for qualified youth, The Venturing Academy and The SPL Leadership Academy.

Come hear Terry Fossum, Eagle Scout, former Air Force pilot, author, and reality show winner, speak at the University of Scouting opening session, telling his story of how Scouting values shaped his life. More information.
