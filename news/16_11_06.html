Re-Charter Today

Every unit must re-charter each year. On time re-charters are much easier than dealing with an expired charter. Start early and it's not overwhelming, wait until the last minute and it's very hard on everyone! Help is available, your unit commissioner can assist with the process. If you are not directly responsible for the re-charter process you can help. Every adult must have a current youth protection training card. Everyone needs to pay their membership fees. Don't wait, get started now! Recharter Checklist

Face to Face Youth Protection Training , Tuesday November 15, 2016

November 15th, 7:00 to 9:00 PM you are invited to take face-to-face Youth Protection Training (F2F YPT) at Spring Creek Elementary. It could not be more convenient! This training is REQUIRED for adults who will participate in overnight scouting events that are 3 or more nights long. It is also HIGHLY RECOMMENDED for all adults who participate in scouting events or weekend campouts. Yes, you can take YPT online, but that will not meet all of the requirements, so bottom line is that this face-to-face session will satisfy ALL YPT requirements for TWO YEARS. More information
