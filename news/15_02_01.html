Camp Card Sales

This is a Risk Free Unit Sales project where all Scouts can earn their way to Camp, Philmont, other activities or simply support unit program or supply needs. Each Camp Card will sell for only $5.00 to the general public and will have multiple food, service and entertainment discounts for the customer to use throughout the year. The participating unit will keep 50% commission for each Camp Card sold! Click here for more information on Camp Card Sales.

Sign up today! The sale starts February 17th!
