A Look Back

It’s a bit of a slow news week, so I thought I would talk about scouting past. I fear repeating myself and for those who have heard these stories before, I apologize. My scouting life began back in a small town in Ohio and while many things are different today, some are very much the same.

As a cub scout we played games and worked on crafts. No camping, but we did have fun. As a scout I worked on advancement and attended summer camp. Woodland Trails Scout Reservation is still hosting scouts today more than 50 years later. I completed the mile swim in the camp pool and earned my patch. I got my first taste of scouting and except for a few years in college, I have been scouting ever since.

Scouting has helped me tremendously with life skills and I can never completely repay the gift of time, talent and treasure provided by so many people years ago. As you can tell, I am a believer in the program because it does make a difference.

Scouting is an investment in the future of our children, it is not about entertainment!

Introduction to Leadership Skills for Troops

ILST (Introduction to Leadership Skills for Troops) is scheduled for April 30-May 1, 2022, at Camp Wisdom. This is for any Scout with a leadership position on the Patrol Leader Council. The course teaches troop organization, leadership skills, how to teach using EDGE and is designed to teach by using games, reflections and other methods to engage youth. Scout's Own is included in the course to be a guide to scouts who serve as Chaplain's aide. Reference material for Scouts is the Patrol Leader's Handbook. On-line Registration. Contact Bill Wilson if you have questions. wrw@billwilsonlaw.com

Who: Youth desiring to be trained in the Leadership Skills required for their positions.
What: The purpose of the ILST course is to teach Scouts with leadership positions about their new roles and how to most effectively reach success in that role. ILST is the first course in the series of leadership training offered to Scouts and is a replacement for Troop Leadership Training. This next course in the series is National Youth Leadership Training.
When: April 30-May 2-2022. Arrive at 8:00 A .M. on April 30 2022. Dismiss at 11:00 AM on May 1, 2022.
Where: Camp Wisdom
