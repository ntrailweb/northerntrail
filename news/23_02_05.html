Camporee is Coming

Every year Northern Trail hosts a Camporee at one of the Circle Ten Scout camps. This year camporee will be held at Trevor Rees-Jones Scout Camp near Athens Texas the weekend of April 14-16, 2023. This year the district will be teaming with a few other districts and this will make for an even better and bigger camporee than in years past.

What is camporee? Camporee is an opportunity to demonstrate scout skills, observe and visit with other units and see how they manage their campouts. The event is capped off with a camp fire program with all of the units present. Usually there are skits and other entertainment. The evening program will usually conclude with an Order of the Arrow call out ceremony.

Expect comfortable temperatures, but there is always the chance for a little rain, but this rarely dampens the activities very much.

If your unit has not participated in a camporee recently, now is the time to change that and plan on attending this year. Expect a great time and a memorable experience for your scouts.More information and unit registration.

Venturing Rendezvous

Venturing Rendezvous is scheduled for March 18, 2023 at Camp Wisdom. Camping is available for Crews and Ships wishing to do so, just reach out once you are registered for Rendezvous and we'll assign you to a campsite.

Rendezvous is the spring event which brings all the Venturers, Sea Scouts, and Explorers together for fun and fellowship. If you are 14 but not part of a Crew or Ship, reach out to a nearby Crew and come as their guest. If you need to find a Crew or you have questions, please feel free to contact Joe Tavares, VOA Advisor for Circle Ten (and a member of this District) for more details. Registration is found on Black Pug at https://scoutingevent.com/571-2023Rendezvous
