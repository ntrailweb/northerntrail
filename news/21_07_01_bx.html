High Adventure/Venturing Forum -- Tuesday, Aug. 21st

This month's program is Wilderness Survival and discussion topics are Summit Shakedown recap and Winter Camp for Venturers. The Forum is an introduction to resources and opportunity for networking, designed to benefit all Venturing leaders (adult and youth), leaders of older scouts and Varsity Teams, and anyone else interested in High Adventure and Venturing-level programs and activities. It meets on the 3rd Tuesday each month, beginning at 7:30 p.m., in Room 2F at the First United Methodist Church of Richardson. Check the website for more information.
