Oak Leaf Training for Scouts

Oak Leaf is a junior leader experience designed to supplement Troop level training and prepare Scouts for NYLT (National Youth Leadership Training). It is for Boy Scout youth, First Class Rank or above who will be serving in a leadership capacity within their Troop. Minimum age is 12 years old at time of course. Registration now available. Seats are limited and the class will fill. Get your scout signed up today.

National Youth Leadership Training (NYLT)

National Youth Leadership Training is an intense, 5 ½ day, outdoor leadership training experience for youth troop, team and crew leaders. The course goal is to prepare young men and women to become superior leaders in their troop, crew, district, council, and other organizations they belong to now, and in the future. Continue reading

Northern Trail Training Opportunities

Day of Training for Adults in Scouting Saturday 9/26/2015

Time to get signed up for training. Every parent and adult leader should come to training. Parents will benefit by understanding the aims and methods of the scout program and leaders will gain valable insights on how to be more effective. Training includes, Cubmaster, Den Leader, Committee member, Face-to-Face YPT and more. Unit leaders, send the parents of your scouts to training! Registration now available. (All day) Epiphany Episcopal Church More information and registration.

Boy Scout Leader Specific Training, Begins 10/7/15

If you have a son in Boy Scouts, this training is for you. In just two evenings you will learn how the program works and how to help your son get the most out of scouting. If you are a leader and work directly with scouts this training is required. The class meets 2 times. Registration now available. More information and registration.

Intro to Outdoor Leader Skills, Weekend October 23rd

This is an follow on to the Boy Scout Leader Specific training above and takes the training outdoors on an actual campout. Participants will take of the roles of scouts and learn by doing. You will have a great time and come away with new skills and build your scouting network. More information.
