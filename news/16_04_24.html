Twilight Camp 2016 Get signed up today!

Time to get registered for Twilight Camp. June 6-10. Sign up for Parent / Walking Den leader orientation and face-to-face YPT classes. Camp would not be possible without parents who attend camp as walking den leaders. All leaders must have face-to-face youth protection training. This training is valid for 2 years, so for many leaders the training has expired. Training available May 3rd, 10th, 17th, 24th and 31st. Check website for registration and locations. This year's theme is Cub Scout C.S.I.
Cub Scouts $85. Tigers $45 Wed-Fri. Walking Den leaders $10. More information and Registration on the website. www.ntrail.org/twilight

Staff needed for Twilight Camp

Those of us who have staffed Twilight camp know that the experience is very rewarding. It's fun and you have the satisfaction of making a difference in the lives of the participants. Both adult and youth staff are needed. Face-to-face YPT is required for staff 18 years and older. Sign up today! Staff Registration Form

Oak Leaf Spring 2016, 5/21-5/22

Oak Leaf is a junior leader experience designed to supplement Troop level training and prepare Scouts for NYLT (National Youth Leadership Training). It is for Boy Scout youth, First Class Rank or above who will be serving in a leadership capacity within their Troop. Minimum age is 12 years old at time of course. Registration now open! Space is limited, don't miss out!

National Youth Leadership Training (NYLT)

National Youth Leadership Training is an intense, 5 ½ day, outdoor leadership training experience for youth troop, team and crew leaders. The course goal is to prepare young men and women to become superior leaders in their troop, crew, district, council, and other organizations they belong to now, and in the future.   Continue reading
