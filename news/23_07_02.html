Richardson Flags Needs Help

The Richardson Rotary Club has been running a project for several years which places flags in neighborhoods. It a scholarship fund raising project and about 1/2 of the subscriptions fees goes for scholarships.

It's a great program and they have partnered with Scout units and other groups to help place and pick up the flags. Groups get compensated $1.25 per for each pickup or placement of a flag. So for example if you put out and pickup 100 flags you get $250 for your group. It's a great way to raise money for your unit and perform community service.

If your unit would like to help with this worthwhile project, please contact Tim Quilici, Richardson Rotary Club Richardson Flags co-chair 214-450-3079 / tim@flagmapper.com

Wood Badge 141 & 142

Wood Badge is the Boy Scouts of America's ultimate leadership training designed to meet the advanced leadership needs of Scouters.

Wood Badge brings together leaders from all areas of Scouting - Cub Scouting, Scouts BSA and Venturing. Our mission is to inspire and train adults through world class leadership training that sets an example for youth empowerment.

Wood Badge 141 will take place at Philmont Scout Range August 6-12, 2023. Registration deadline is July 27, 2023.

Wood Badge 142 will be held at Camp Constantin the weekends of October 20-22, 2023 and November 4-5, 2023. Registration deadline October 10, 2023.
