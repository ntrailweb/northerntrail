Training Opportunities

Boy Scout Leader Specific, Starts March 23

This training is fun and informative. If you want your scout to get the most out of the program, you need to attend this course. This course for parents and adult leaders in Boy Scout units. This course will teach you how the program works, about organization, operation, outdoor program and role of adult leaders in a troop. The class meets 2 times. Continue reading

Intro to Outdoor Leader Skills, April 8-10

Everyone without exception who attends this course really enjoys the experience. Learn by doing. Participate in an actual Boy Scout weekend campout. You will learn first-hand how a Boy Scout Troop operates in the field. You will see scouting from the perspective of a scout and really understand the program. This course is taught in conjunction with the 2 classroom sessions offered a few weeks earlier. It's is strongly recommended that you participate in both. Advance preparation and signup required. BALOO Training For Cub Scout Leaders Saturday April 9, 2016

Basic Adult Leader Outdoor Orientation training is an introduction to the Cub Scout outdoor program. BSA’s Cub Scout level camping policies will be taught along with the necessary tools to help units carry out a successful camping experience.

BSA requires that at least one adult on a Pack overnighter be BALOO trained. Webelos Den Leaders camping as a den must be BALOO trained. Continue Reading
