Youth Protection Training Deadline

A priority in scouting is the safety of every participant, both youth and adult. To this end it is required that every adult who is registered, must complete a youth protection training session. Every registered adult must have a current youth protection card to complete the annual re-charter process. This required training is good for two years. Either the on-line or face-to-face training will meet this requirement. Online training is available here: my.scouting.org/. The training does not take very long and is not difficult.
