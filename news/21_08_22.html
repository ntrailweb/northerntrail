Training for Scouts

ILST is Introduction to Leadership Skills for Troops. October 8-10 at Camp Wisdom. This is for any Scouts BSA Patrol Leader Council member. Course has replaced Oak Leaf. Teaches troop organization, leadership skills, how to teach using EDGE. The course is designed to teach by using games and other methods to engage youth. Scout's Own is included in the course to be a guide to scouts who serve as Chaplain's aide. Reference material for Scouts is the Patrol Leader's Handbook. On-line Registration.

The Gathering, September 18

Scouts and Venturers, aged 14-20, are invited to attend The Gathering at Clements Scout Ranch - Trevor Rees-Jones Camp. The Gathering will be held Saturday, September 18 from 9-6. This is a day-long event full of fun for Venturers and those interested in Venturing. Would you like to drive a bobcat loader? Climb the TRJ Tower? Shoot pistols and shotguns? Just hang out with older kids in scouting? All that and more will be available. Space is limited to the first 250 participants so watch for registration to open on our camp reservations site: https://scoutingevent.com/571-2021VenturingGathering.

A BBQ lunch is provided as part of the registration. Normal registration documents are required (AB health form, COVID forms, etc.). Cost will be $25 for early birds and $35 after 9/12/2021. If you need a sponsoring Crew, reach out to Joe Tavares and he will get you in touch with the Crew's President and Program VP.
