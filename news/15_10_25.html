Re-Charter Clinic

It's re-charter time. Every unit must complete the re-charter process annually. The deadline for all units is fast approaching. If you get started early, it's not all that difficult. Wait until the last minute and it can be a real headache. If you are not responsible for the process, you can help by getting your dues, any applications or needed youth protection forms into your unit chair as soon as possible. If you are responsible for your unit re-charter, help is available. A re-charter clinic will be held on December 5th at the RISD Administration building at 7:00 PM.

Here is a handy checklist which will help you to be prepared. You can get your paperwork checked and turned in without having to go to the service center. Continue reading

Courts of Honor and Blue and Gold Banquets

Scouting depends on volunteers and contributions to make the program run. Please take a moment to schedule a time for a presenter to come to your Court of Honor or Blue and Gold Banquet and make a short presentation about Friends of Scouting. It will only take a about 15 minutes. It 's essential that our local Circle Ten Council have adequate funds to implement this great program with the necessary camps, professional staff and service centers. Oak Leaf Training for Scouts November 7-8,2015

Oak Leaf is a junior leader experience designed to supplement Troop level training and prepare Scouts for NYLT (National Youth Leadership Training). It is for Boy Scout youth, First Class Rank or above who will be serving in a leadership capacity within their Troop. Minimum age is 12 years old at time of course. Registration now available.
