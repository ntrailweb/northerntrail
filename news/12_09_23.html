A Day of Training -- Saturday 9/29/2012 -- Register Today

This is truly a "Day of Training." Opportunities include:

  • Life / Eagle, that last step to Eagle and be a stumbling block. Learn the facts about the process and the resources available.
  • Troop Committee Training, there is more to making a troop work than a great scoutmaster. He or she needs to be backed up by a well oiled machine. Learn how to help fill this vital role.
  • Chartered Organization Representative Training, an often overlooked position is that of the COR. A great relationship between the and the chartered organization will help a unit run smoothly. Learn about the role of the COR.
  • Merit Badge Counselor, Sometimes it can be difficult to locate counselors for your scouts. This course explains the process and procedures so you can insure that there will be no unexpected difficulties.
  • Face to Face Youth Protection, adults who wish to participate in summer camp or Twilight Camp will need this course. Complete it now well in advance of summer camp.
  • Cub Scout Leader Specific Training, separate sessions for every position in the cub scout program; Cubmaster, Committee Chair, Tiger Den Leader, Wolf/Bear Den Leader and Webelos Den Leader.

Den Chief Training, -- Saturday 9/29/2012 -- More information

Den Chief's are mature boy scouts who assist Den Leaders as boy leaders. The Den Chief is a critical member of the Scouting Leadership Team. Den Chief Training is intended to build enthusiasm for the job and teach the skills the Den Chief will need to be successful. This training combines fun and enjoyment with an excellent character building experience.
