Commissioners Corner

Have you planned your scouting year?

If you have not planned your calendar scouting year yet, it's really important to get that done now. A group of parents and leaders can get together for an hour or two and really help plan a spectacular year. By planning the year out now and publishing it for all of the families, you will have much better turn-out at your events. Read more.

Northern Trail to Adventure, Saturday, 10/5/2013

Our first big event of the year is Northern Trail to Adventure (NT2A), which takes place at Camp Wisdom. Packs are invited to spend the night in the camp area. Archery, BB guns, Caves, Rope Bridge, Sir Dee Castle, Trading Post, Pirate Ships, Campfire, and much more. Use this event as a recruiting tool at your "School Night for Scouting." This is an excellent opportunity to showcase Cub Scouting to your new recruits. Check out pictures from last year.

When: October 5, 2013 - 10 AM  (Don't miss our evening activities!)

Cost: $20 per Scout and $15 for Adults or Siblings during Early Bird Registration until Sept 22nd.

Camping: Plan to camp with us Saturday night.  Reserve online.
