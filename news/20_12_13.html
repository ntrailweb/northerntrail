Winter camp December 27-31 Waitlisted

Circle Ten Council’s annual Winter Camp at Trevor Rees-Jones Scout Camp is a special jamboree-style camping event for Scouts BSA troops, Venturing Crews and Sea Scout Ships. It is the largest annual event put on by Circle Ten Council and is staffed by Circle Ten’s Mikanakawa Lodge of the Order of the Arrow. Units camp jamboree style with multiple units in a single campsite. The chance to make new friends from outside your immediate Scouting circle is one of the key rewards. Food is prepared by each unit in their campsites (with the exception of the optional closing night BBQ).

Youth Protection Training is Required

This is not optional. If you don't have a current card, you cannot participate in any scouting activity virtual or in-person. Lack of current YPT training will stymie your unit re-charter. No charter, no unit, no advancement. Please don't delay get this done. It only takes about an hour to complete. Go to my.scouting.org/ If you have trouble making it work speak up. We will not let you fail.

Spring NYLT

National Youth Leadership Training in 2021. First course will be during spring break: Match 3-19, 2021. NYLT is an exciting, action-packed program designed for councils to provide youth members with leadership skills and experience they can use in their home troops and in other situations demanding leadership of self and others.

The NYLT course centers around the concepts of what a leader must BE, what he must KNOW, and what he must DO. The key elements are then taught with a clear focus on HOW TO. The skills come alive during the week as the patrol goes on a Quest for the Meaning of Leadership. Registration opens December 31,2020. More information.
