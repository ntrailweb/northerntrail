  Scouting Blog

Cub Mobile Derby is a crashing success!

With the help of Boy Scouts and their leaders, the incredible weather, and passionate participants, the Cub Mobile Derby was an incredible success this year. The cooler weather was welcomed by all. Never has it been this cool for the Derby in the middle of July. Plus, this event showed how Boy Scouts and Cub Scouts can come together to have fun and provide a model for the younger Scouts. It's been my desire to get the Boy Scouts involved for years. Thanks, Troop 570!
