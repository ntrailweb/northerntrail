Obey the Scout Law

The twelve points of the Scout Law are guidelines that can lead you toward wise choices. When you obey the Scout Law, other people will respect you for the way you live, and you will respect yourself.
Scouting reinforces these basic values.

Training Makes a Huge Difference

Is your unit struggling? Not getting the results you want? The key is training, strive to get every parent in your unit trained this fall. It will benefit your unit in several ways. Some of these parents will become more active in your unit and the rest will understand the program and be able to help their child progress and they will be more supportive of you. Details on upcoming training will be posted soon, start working now to sell the parents of your scouts on training!

National Youth Leadership Training (NYLT)

National Youth Leadership Training is an intense, 5 ½ day, outdoor leadership training experience for youth troop, team and crew leaders. The course goal is to prepare young men and women to become superior leaders in their troop, crew, district, council, and other organizations they belong to now, and in the future. Continue reading
