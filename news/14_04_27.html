Twilight Camp is Almost Here

Have you registered yet? If not! HURRY! Early bird registration ends April 30th and offers a great discount and ensures that your son will get a shirt and patch.

If you've never been to Twilight Camp, it's an excellent way for your cubs to have a lot of fun while advancing on their Cub Scout trail! It is the perfect place for Cubs to earn belt loop and rank advancement while getting out of the house for a week with their friends. The Cub Scouts will participate in daily activities that include BB guns, archery, crafts, Scout skills, field games and sports, and more.
