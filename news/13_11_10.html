Recognition of Adult Volunteers

Adult Awards

Scouters should be recognized for all that they do for the scout program at any level, Cubs, Boy Scouts, Varsity, and Venturing. They are honored at the Northern Trail Annual Banquet in January and can only be recognized when one or several other scouters nominate them for an awards. Now is the time to nominate someone for one of these District Awards.

The awards are, The District Award of Merit, Robert E. Little Award, Colonel Henry Crown Leadership and Service Award, and Y.E.S. Your Extraordinary Service Award. See ntrail.org/?q=content/individual-scouting-awards for all the details for each of these. Nomination are due Dec 1, 2013.
