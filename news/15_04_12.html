Courteous

The fifth point of the Scout Law is; "A Scout is Courteous." A Scout is polite to everyone regardless of age or position. He knows that using good manners makes it easier for people to get along. Scouting reinforces this basic value.

Youth Protection Training is Required

Face to Face Youth protection is available at the Fairview office during April. More Face to Face YPT in May at First United Methodist Church. All leaders are required to have Youth Protection Training. The on-line version is available at myscouting.org, but the Face to Face version is required for Adult leaders at Twilight and Overnight Camps.

Activities For Your Scouts

There are a number of upcoming activities and it's time to get signed up. These include: Twilight Camp for Cub Scouts. Parent -N- Pal Weekend for Cubscouts. Webelos Resident Camp for Webelos Scouts. All of these activities require advanced sign up. Don't make the job of the organizers more difficult than it needs to be. Sign up promptly.
