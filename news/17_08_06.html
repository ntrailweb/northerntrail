Roundtables This Week

Cub Scout, Boy Scout and Venturing Roundtables will be held on Tuesday August 8th at 7:00PM. Location will be the First United Methodist Church of Richardson. Map

The topic for the Roundtables will be recruiting.

Your last chance for an open forum on Recruiting Boy Scouts before this year's Join Scouting Night. We hear about your diminishing numbers and low-to-no recruiting years. This is your "take action" chance to beef up your membership. Please Join us Tuesday at 7:00 PM at the First United Methodist Church in Richardson Rm 230. You're bound to take away some worthwhile ideas, and you probably have some great ideas to share with other leaders. And remember, if it wasn't important for you to attend, wouldn't ask.

2017 Northern Trail Video Contest

Here is a fun project for your unit of any type. Create a either (1) a 30 to 60 second public service announcement or (2) a 3- to 5-minute mini documentary that could be used for recruiting Cub Scouts, Boy Scouts, Varsity Scouts or Venturers. Use this as an opportunity to complete a requirements for the Webelos Moviemaking elective or the Moviemaking Merit Badge. More information
