Kind

The sixth point of the Scout Law. A Scout is Kind. A Scout knows there is strength in being gentle. He treats others as he wants to be treated. Without good reason, he does not harm or kill any living thing.. Scouting reinforces this basic value.

Mountaineer Weekends at the Summit

Mountaineer Weekends are the perfect way to get a feel for The Summit in a short period of time, whether passing through or just making a weekend of it.

Get a taste of the adventure and action sports opportunities available at the BSA’s newest national base — The Summit Bechtel Reserve — located in Southern West Virginia. The Mountaineer Weekend program gives you access to all venues in the Scott Summit Center. This area is a microcosm of the Summit with sample versions of each of the exciting activities found elsewhere on the property. Continue reading.

Hone your Wood Badge skills this Summer at the Summit!

With a focus on Servant Leadership, the Summit Leadership Challenge takes your leadership skills to the next level. Use this week long experience to refine your Wood Badge skills and learn from BSA National Instructors. Through a series of practical exercises and reflection, your skills will be improved.
