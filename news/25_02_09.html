Thank you, Northern Trail, Thank you!

Yesterday, February 8, was the 115th anniversary of Scouting America and Scouts across the nation celebrated with a day of service and Northern Trail was no exception. We had four service projects across the district and a fantastic turnout.

While picking up litter at Breckenridge Park and Spring Creek Nature Area may not seem exciting, it was very worthwhile and productive. And the landscaping cleanup at First United Methodist repaid the church not only for hosting district meetings and events but several units. And collecting food and clothing for Network benefited our neighbors. Next week I’ll have a list of the units that participated along with the amount of service provided.

As a final note, someone left a pair of deerskin colored work gloves at Breckenridge Park. If they’re yours let me know.

Once again thanks to everyone who participated.

Hank Voegtle
Event coordinator. hjv3@outlook.com

WFA Wilderness First Aid

Wilderness First Aid is required for attending all BSA High Adventure bases - Philmont - requires 3 with WFA; Northern Tier - 1; Sea Base - 1; Summit -1 recommends having 2. and in general recommended for all scouting activities beyond one hour of medical help. I will have 4 classes this spring.

Mar 7 - 9
Apr 4 - 6
May 16-19

Classes: Friday, night; Saturday, all day; Sunday, afternoon Cost: $90
CPR/AED is required and will be offered the Thursday night before each class.
For the application and more information contact Mike Reisinger, 972-907-8949, mj1234r@sbcglobal.net

Youth Protection Training (YPT)

Avoid the rush, check your card and get it done. Training is valid for two years and you must retake the online course. If you are out of date, it is full stop. Go to my.scouting.org for this and other training opportunities. Don't forget Hazardous Weather Training and Range Activities Safety Training.

On-Line Training

A huge amount of training material is available online, including position training and Youth Protection. Access your account here. If you don't have an account, it is easy to set one up. You can access your training records and set up a complete training program.

Venturing Rendezvous is happening April 26, 2025. SAVE THE DATE!

If you are not going to Conclave, come to Rendezvous. All Venturing age-eligible youth are invited. If you are part of a Crew, Ship, Post, or Troop and are 13 1/2, you are eligible to attend (Scoutmasters, sorry but we cannot have younger scouts attend). If a unit wants to bring new recruits outside of Scouting America, they can attend the day event on Saturday with a parent-signed waiver. Camping for registered members is available Friday and Saturday night. Rendezvous is a Camp Wisdom Adventure.

The theme will be Medieval Rendezvous: Journey to the Middle Ages.

The Venturing Officers Association (VOA) will be putting flyers out soon with event information and pricing. A link to black pug is https://scoutingevent.com/571-2025Rendezvous. Please stay tuned for updates.
