Circle Ten Council Annual Recognition Banquet - April 5, 2018

Congratulations to all the Northern Trail Scouts who earned their Eagle Scout rank in 2017! What a wonderful accomplishment! Parents, are you aware that Circle Ten Council honors all the previous year's Eagle Scouts at the annual banquet, and that their attendance is complimentary, for them and their Scout Master? When they receive their invitation in the mail, please RSVP and make plans to attend. They will be recognized with the whole John Stewart 2017 Class of Eagles, as well as be included in the group photo that will hang in the Murchison Scouting Center, and receive a special commemorative Council Strip Patch. Eagles must attend to receive the patch.
