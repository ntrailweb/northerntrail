Passing of Norm Meador

Norm Meador passed away Saturday morning July 7, 2012. He was an active part of Troop 890 for more than 40 years. He will be missed. Arrangements

District Cubmobile Derby, Saturday morning, July 21, 2012

Detailed information on this event is now available.  As in past years it will be held at Flag Pole Hill.  It's guaranteed to be a blast.  Get your den together and build a car and race!  It's a great summertime activity. Each Cub can build their own car, or a den, patrol, or pack can work together to make a team car.  More details on the website.
