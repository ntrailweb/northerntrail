Training

Leaders, send your parents to Training -- One of the best ways to get help with your unit activities is to send your parents to training. It's an easy sell, not a big time commitment and they will be better prepared to help their own scout get the most out of the program and by the way, it's fun. The exact details for the fall training schedule are still being worked out, but a full suite of courses will be available, both classroom and outdoor for all the programs.
University of Scouting -- Saturday January 4, 2014. Mark your calender and plan to attend. There will be something of interest for every adult in your unit!
NYLT -- Highest level of youth leadership training available for boy scouts, varsity, venturing and sea scouts. Designed to complement Wood Badge training for adults. See NYLT website for more details.http://www.c10nylt.org/ "Every youth deserves the opportunity to be a trained leader!"
