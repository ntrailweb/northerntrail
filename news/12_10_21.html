Adult Recognition -- Deadlines approaching!

Y.E.S. award nominations are due December 1st. There are adult volunteers in your unit deserving of recognition. If you know of someone in your unit who should be recognized, please consider them for a Y.E.S. (Your Extraordinary Service) nomination. More information.

District Award of Merit nominations are due December 1st. This award is a council award presented by districts or Venturing divisions in the same manner that the Silver Beaver is a national award presented by councils. More information.

Robert E. Little Award nominations are due December 1st. The Northern Trail District committee presents the Robert E. Little Service Award to a scouter who has rendered outstanding service to the scouting movement at the unit and/or district level. More information.

Silver Beaver Nominations are due November 1st. This is a National Scouting Award presented by the local council to adult volunteers who have made significant contributions to the scouting movement over a number of years. More information

More Training Opportunities

BALOO (Basic Adult Leader Outdoor Orientation), Saturday, 2012, November 10 - 8:00am. Taking Cub Scouts outdoors is a great way to keep them interested in scouting. Get the proper training on how to have safe and enjoyable outings. More information.

OWLS (Outdoor Skills for Webelos Leaders), Saturday, 2012, November 10 - 8:00am to Sunday, 2012, November 11 - 12:00pm. Webelows are transitioning to Boy Scouts and that means overnight camping. Learn the ins and outs of taking scouts camping by participating in an actual campout. Oh, you will have a lot of fun in the process. Advance preparation and registration required. More information.

Wilderness Advanced First Aid Two weekends, 2/8-10/2013 & 2/15-17/2013. Serious 40 hour course, you will be much better prepared for emergencies far from outside help after taking this course. More Information

University of Scouting Saturday, January 5,2013 Southern Methodist University. The University of Scouting is a Scout leader's learning adventure! It is an action-packed, fun-filled single day of supplemental training where you choose the sessions you wish to take. More information.
