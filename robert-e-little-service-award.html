Robert E. Little Service Award

The Northern Trail District committee presents the Robert E. Little Service Award to a scouter who has rendered outstanding service to the scouting movement at the unit and/or district level. It is given primarily for dedicated service, personal effort, and unselfish interest contributed beyond the immediate responsibilities of the position or positions held over a period of years.

The nomination form may be found here. Nominations must be completed and submitted to the District Awards and Recognition Chairman by December 31st.

See the See district leadership page for name and email address for the current Awards and Recognition Chairperson.
